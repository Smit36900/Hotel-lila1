SOUTHERN WARMTH & COOL VIBES

Welcome to our modern haven centrally poised amid the charms of Wilmington, an alluring coastal North Carolina town. Hotel Lela places you just four miles from Wilmington’s Historic Riverfront District, six miles from scenic Wrightsville Beach, and two miles from UNCW.

The name Lela evokes a free-spirited nature, a sense of grace, and an appreciation for art and beauty. No other name better captures what Hotel Lela offers. Enjoy a serene aesthetic with vibrant pops of color, an outdoor saltwater pool surrounded by cabanas, two golf swing suites, a standout signature restaurant, and all the warmth this historic yet progressive town is known for.
